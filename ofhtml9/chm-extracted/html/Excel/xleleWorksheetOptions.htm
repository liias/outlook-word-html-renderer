WorksheetOptions Element (Microsoft Excel)

Contains the properties of a worksheet.

Contained In

ExcelWorksheet, XML

Subelements

ActiveCol, ActivePane, ActiveRow, AlternateExpressionEvaluation, AlternateFormulaEntry, ApplyAutomaticOutlineStyles, CodeName, DefaultColumnWidth, DefaultRowHeight, DisplayFormulas, DisplayRightToLeft, DoNotDisplayGridlines, DoNotDisplayHeadings, DoNotDisplayOutline, DoNotDisplayZeros, ExcelWorksheetType, FilterOn, FitToPage, FreezePanes, FrozenNoSplit, GridlineColorIndex, IntlMacro, LeftColumnRightPane, Name, NoSummaryColumnsRightDetail, NoSummaryRowsBelowDetail, PageBreakZoom, Panes, Print, ProtectContents, ProtectObjects, ProtectScenarios, RangeSelection, Selected, ShowPageBreakZoom, SplitHorizontal, SplitVertical, StandardWidth, TopRowBottomPane, TopRowVisible, TransitionExpressionEvaluation, TransitionFormulaEntry, Visible, Zoom

Remarks

For a workbook, all subelements are optional. For the PivotList component, the Panes and Selected subelements are required. When the data comes from a PivotList component, the DefaultRowHeight subelement is also required.
