ExternName Element (Microsoft Excel)

Contains information about a DDE or OLE link, or a referenced name that is defined in another workbook.

Contained In

SupBook

Subelements

Cf, Formula, Name, OLELink, WantAdvise, WantPict

Remarks

The Cf, Formula, and Name subelements are required, but all other subelements are optional.
