ShowLeaderLines Element (Microsoft Excel)

Specifies whether leader lines drawn from a slice's center to its data labels are shown in a pie chart.

Contained In

DataLabels, LabelEntry

Remarks

If <x:ShowLeaderLines/> is specified, leader lines are drawn.

This element can be specified for pie, 3-D pie, bar of pie, and pie of pie charts. The LeaderLines element specifies the line format and style.
