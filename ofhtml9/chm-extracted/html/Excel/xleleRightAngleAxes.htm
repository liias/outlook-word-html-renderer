RightAngleAxes Element (Microsoft Excel)

Specifies whether axes are drawn at right angles (parallel projection) regardless of elevation, rotation, and perspective values.

Contained In

View3D

Remarks

If <x:RightAngleAxes/> is specified, the axes are drawn at right angles. The chart is drawn so that it's closer in size and appearance to an equivalent 2-D chart, rather than with the perspective of a 3-D chart.
