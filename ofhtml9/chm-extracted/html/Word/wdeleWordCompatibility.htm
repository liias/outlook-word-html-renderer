WordCompatibility Element (Microsoft Word)

Specifies a document's compatibility settings.

Contained In

WordDocument

Subelements

AdjustLineHeightInTable, AlignTablesRowByRow, AutoSpaceLikeWord95, BalanceSingleByteDoubleByteWidth, ConvMailMergeEsc, DoNotExpandShiftReturn, DoNotLeaveBackslashAlone, DoNotUseHTMLParagraphAutoSpacing, FootnoteLayoutLikeWW8, ForgetLastTabAlignment, LayoutRawTableWidth, LayoutTableRowsApart, LineWrapLikeWord6, MWSmallCaps, NoColumnBalance, NoExtraLineSpacing, NoLeading, NoSpaceRaiseLower, NoTabHangIndent, OrigWordTableRules, PrintBodyTextBeforeHeader, PrintColBlack, ShapeLayoutLikeWW8, ShowBreaksInFrames, SpaceForUL, SpacingInWholePoints, SubFontBySize, SuppressBottomSpacing, SuppressSpBfAfterPgBrk, SuppressTopSpacing, SuppressTopSpacingMac5, SuppressTopSpacingWP, SwapBordersFacingPages, TransparentMetafiles, TruncateFontHeight, ULTrailSpace, UseFELayout, UsePrinterMetrics, UseWord97LineBreakingRules, WPJustification, WPSpaceWidth, WW6BorderRules, WrapTrailSpaces
