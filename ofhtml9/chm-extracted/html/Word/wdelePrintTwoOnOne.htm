PrintTwoOnOne Element (Microsoft Word)

Specifies whether a document is set up to print two pages per sheet.

Contained In

WordDocument

Remarks

If <w:PrintTwoOnOne/> is specified, two pages are printed per sheet.

See Also

NoLineBreaksAfter, NoLineBreaksBefore, PunctuationKerning, UseFELayout
