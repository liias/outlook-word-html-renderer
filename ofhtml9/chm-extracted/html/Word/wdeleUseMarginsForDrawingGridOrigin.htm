UseMarginsForDrawingGridOrigin Element (Microsoft Word)

Specifies whether the upper-left corner of the document area within the margins is used as a grid's origin. 

Contained In

WordDocument

Remarks

If <w:UseMarginsForDrawingGridOrigin/> is specified, the grid does not use the document's margin settings for its origin.
